Built CyberQuest from scratch as sole architect and developer — a gamified cybersecurity training platform for enterprises. Implements OTP-based passwordless auth, role-based content personalization for administrative, technical, and managerial profiles, and AI-powered contextual feedback with automatic certificate generation after each module. Architected for regulatory compliance use cases aligned with ISO 27001, COBIT, and OWASP training frameworks.
